Art Methods with Pens

1. Stippling

Stippling is an art technique that uses countless tiny dots to create shading and texture. By varying the density and distribution of dots, artists can achieve beautiful gradients and intricate details. This method is perfect for pens because of their precision and control.

2. Cross-Hatching

Cross-hatching involves drawing sets of parallel lines that overlap at angles to build up tone and texture. This classic pen technique is widely used in illustration and sketching to create depth and shadow.
